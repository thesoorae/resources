Welcome to Lagolupus

A Game of Resources and Consumption

How to Play

Click on the field to watch the populations grow and die. Then tweak the controls on the right to get the ecosystem to achieve realistic harmony. How does changing one parameter affect populations? Monitor the stats panel at the bottom to see how populations are growing and dying out.

Rules

  1. At every step of the game, Predators (black) and Prey (pink) burn energy and Grass level grows (0-5)
  2. Predators eat Prey if they are next to them. Prey eat the max amount of grass on each square.
  3. As grass level for each square gets lower, green gets lighter.
  4. Once animals reach reproductive rate and food level, they have a 50% chance of reproducing at each step
  5. More detailed rules can be found on the github page.
